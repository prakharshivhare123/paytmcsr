CSR ACTIVITY OF PAYTM

Paytm pledged to donate Rs 500 crore to the PM CARES Fund to support the country's fight against COVID-19 pandemic. "We are honoured to do our duty to aid the government in all the relief measures being taken to fight the Corona pandemic. We hope our users wholeheartedly donate to PM CARES fund and help save lives," Madhur Deora, President - Paytm, said in a statement. Paytm also Stood for the following.

1 Donation to Bhopal Gas Tragedy Survivors

2 Independence Day Campaign for the future of India- Skill training of youth.

3 Blood Donation Camp.
